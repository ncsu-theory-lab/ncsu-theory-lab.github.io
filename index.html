CS Theory at NCSU

The Computer Science Theory group at NC State University focuses on the fundamental principles of computation, exploring the limits of what can be computed and the efficiency of algorithms. Our active research areas include computational geometry, string algorithms, the role of randomness in computation, cryptography, and computational biology.

If you would like to join our group as a graduate student, please apply to the PhD program in Computer Science at NCSU.

To learn more about upcoming theory events, you can subscribe to the theory group mailing list.
